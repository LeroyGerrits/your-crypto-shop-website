Pricing

Our pricing model is very straightforward. We simply charge a 1% commission on your sales. For every 1000 Digibyte you sell, you receive 990 Digibyte in your supplied wallet address and DGB Commerce receives 10 Digibyte. Transaction fees are on us. It's that simple!

As a merchant using DGB Commerce's platform there are no web server, hosting, web site maintenance or other costs involved. We got you covered. You can focus completely your product catalog, customers and sales. All platform features are available for any registered merchant, there are no required features tucked away behind a complicated licensing model.
