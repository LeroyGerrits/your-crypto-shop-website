About Your Crypto Shop

Your Crypto Shop is an initiative by Leroy Gerrits, a full-stack web developer and former business owner from the Netherlands with many years of experience building e-commerce solutions and SAAS platforms. Being disappointed by the scarcity of utility and real life use cases for cryptocurrencies he decided to put two and two together and create Your Crypto Shop.

While there are existing means for merchants to offer their products and get paid in cryptocurrencies, most of them exist in the form of plugins/add-ons/components for third-party software, charge hefty fees, are tucked away between a myriad of other payment methods and steps in a checkout proces, are cumbersome to use and/or require at least some technical know-how to implement. With Your Crypto Shop, those thresholds are gone and merchants can fully focus on their products and sales once more.

Our mission is to give merchants an intuitive toolbox to easily create a web shop to sell goods and services, and get paid in crypto.
